Flock

This corporate carpooling app was designed to help employees at various companies in the bay area get to work with their peers. The app demonstrates our ability to do some complex math to figure out which drivers are best able to pick up which passengers. We also broke down the Apple Maps API to allow users to see an estimate of where they should be at any given time on their commute to better choose passengers based on their route.

  • Client: Enterprise Distributed (Private)
  • Date: 2014
  • Service: Enterprise iOS App
